Apple Pie Recipe

By Hayato Takai

Prep Time: 30 min   |   Total Time: 3 hrs

Step 1 Step 2 Step 3 Step 4

Ingredients








Step 1

Coat inside of baking tray with butter. Lay a sheet of puff pastry onto the tray and cut off excess.

Step 2

Mix apples, cinnamon, nutmeg, sugar, salt, and lemon juice and lay inside puff pastry

Step 3

Cover apples with puff pastry and add slits for decoration

Step 4

Bake at 375°F for 25 min
